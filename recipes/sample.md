# Chicken Stir Fry

## Ingredients:
- Chicken breast
- Soy sauce
- Broccoli
- Garlic
- Olive oil

## Instructions:
1. Slice the chicken breast.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan.

4. Add chicken and cook thoroughly.
